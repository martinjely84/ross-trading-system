① Startup (every time the bot launches or restarts)

🔒 Singleton enforcement
Scans all running processes. Kills any other main.py instance, then writes its own PID. Guarantees exactly ONE bot — fixes the duplicate-message / duplicate-order problem.
↓
🔄 Reconcile with Alpaca
Compares Alpaca's live positions to what the session knows. Any position the bot doesn't track (an orphan from a crash) gets force-closed — never left unmonitored.
↓
📡 Resume Telegram + scheduler
Loads message offset, starts the job scheduler. Bot is live and listening.

② Morning routine (weekdays only — weekend & holiday aware)

8:00am ET Clean up & arm
Closes any leftover positions from yesterday. Pulls real account balance from Alpaca. Sets risk limits (2% daily max, 0.5% per trade). Resets the day's counters.
↓
8:00am ET Pre-market scan
Finviz finds stocks gapping up 2%+ on volume. Claude AI scores each catalyst 1–10 (earnings, FDA, contract). Scores under 4 dropped. Builds the watchlist ranked A+ / A / B. Also runs a gap-DOWN scan for short candidates.
↓
8:30am ET Watchlist sent to Telegram
You see the day's candidates with gap %, volume, float, and catalyst.

③ Entry logic (9:31–11:00am ET · every 30 seconds)

For each watchlist stock, check the gates in order ↓
Gate 1 — State: armed? not suspended? daily loss limit not hit?
Gate 2 — Already traded? Blocked if stopped out or lost on this stock today. Re-entry only allowed if last trade was profitable AND 20+ min ago.
Gate 3 — Caps: max 5 distinct tickers/day, max 3 open at once (counts open + in-flight).
Gate 4 — Duplicate guards: not already held, not in-flight, not on Alpaca (positions AND open orders), per-ticker buy lock acquired. This is the XOS-fix layer.
Gate 5 — Price valid & in range.
Gate 6 — ENTRY CRITERIA (the edge): price above VWAP · last 1-min candle green · volume > 1.5× prior 5 bars · not over-extended (<5% above VWAP). Fails safe — skips if data missing.
↓ all gates pass
💰 Place market buy on Alpaca
Marks ticker in-flight immediately (before fill confirms). Waits for the actual fill price. Uses the REAL filled quantity (handles partial fills — no oversell). Sizes position to 0.5% account risk.
↓
📐 Set stop & targets from real fill
Stop = 2% below fill · Target 1 = +1.5R · Target 2 = +3R. Logged to session + Telegram. One new trade per 30-sec cycle.

④ Position monitor (every 5 seconds, monitor-only stops)

⚙️ One stop system — the monitor watches price and fires market sells
No broker-held stop orders. This eliminates the double-fire / oversell-into-short bug. Every exit is locked so two cycles can't both sell the same position.
↓ checks each tick
🛑 Stop Loss
Price below stop → sell all. Loss capped ~1R.
📉 VWAP Break
Drops below VWAP (after 3-min grace) → sell all.
🎯 Target 1 (+1.5R)
Sell 50%, move stop to breakeven.
🎯 Target 2 (+3R)
Sell 25%, runner continues.
🏃 Runner Exit
VWAP break / EMA loss / lower-low on runner.
⏱️ Time Stop
15 min, no progress to T1 → warning.

⑤ Window close & reporting

11:00am ET Hard cutoff
Force-closes ALL Alpaca positions (checks real positions, not just session — catches orphans). Trading window closed. Fires once (locked).
↓
12:00pm ET Daily report
One report (locked). Trade breakdown by conviction, real fill prices, correct R-multiples. Claude AI reviews the day and suggests improvements.
↓
3:55pm ET End-of-day safety close
Any position still open (shouldn't be) is closed. Fires once.

🛡️ Safety mechanisms (always on)

Singleton lock
Only one bot can ever run — no duplicate orders or messages.
Daily loss limit (2%)
Trading auto-suspends if cumulative losses hit the limit.
Position & ticker caps
Max 3 open at once, max 5 distinct stocks/day — no runaway buying.
No-revenge rule
Can't re-enter a stock after a stop-out or loss the same day.
Reconcile on restart
Orphaned positions get closed automatically on every boot.
Confirmed fills only
Positions are logged at the real Alpaca fill price & quantity, never an estimate.
Telegram authorization
Only your chat ID can send commands.
15-test regression suite
Every past failure is a permanent test — run before any live session.
